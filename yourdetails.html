Cancellation rights
You have a right to cancel up to 14 days from the date you receive the policy documents. You will be entitled to a refund of premium provided that you have not started a trip to which the insurance applies and you have not made or intend to make a claim. Following the expiry of your 14-day cancellation period you continue to have the right to cancel your policy at any time during its term by contacting us, but no refund of premium will be available. For Annual Multi-Trip policies, the 14-day cancellation period is extended up to the start date of your policy.
Important Information about your policy
Travel insurance provides cover for a wide range of unforeseen or unexpected circumstances that could happen before the start of a holiday or during the trip but does not cover every possible situation or event.
