Gadget cover provides protection of up to £1000 per policy for loss, theft or damage to mobile/smart phones, tablets, laptops, computers, smart watches, games consoles (including handheld consoles), wearable activity trackers, including any accessory items, and drones taken on a trip.
Note: This cover is per policy and not per person. A policy Excess will apply and there may also be a deduction for wear and tear. Cameras are excluded from this cover.

Protect the costs of your trip should you be affected by a Natural Catastrophe like volcanic eruptions (including volcanic ash clouds being carried by the wind) earthquakes or tsunamis occurring within 14 days or your trip and within 20 square kilometres of your destination. Or should a Terrorism event (as declared by the UK government or by the government of the country where the event happened) occurs within 20km of your arrival point and 14 days of your proposed trip date, you can protect the costs of your trip should you decide you no longer want to travel. If Airspace closure delays your departure from the UK or when you are returning to the UK, this additional cover can assist you with any daily costs you may incur as a result of waiting for the Airspace to be re-opened.

Provides additional cover to protect you against certain unforeseen costs should Covid-19 impact you before you travel or whilst you are away. Even if the quarantine requirements change while you are on holiday, this can provide cover for the cost of the accommodation and Covid-19 tests that you were not liable for at the time you booked your trip or purchased your policy (whichever you did last). This cover is not applicable to trips taken solely in the UK.
Cover is available on the ‘Missed Departure from the UK’, ‘Cutting short Your Trip’, ‘Missed Departure and connections on your Return to the UK’ (including an additional daily benefit) and ‘Change in Covid Testing and/or Quarantine Requirements During the Trip’ sections of the policy.(See the Policy Wording for full terms)
